McrFRED Round 6 Martin Rue on JavaScript as a Language

McrFRED Round 6 is confirmed for Thursday 29th August, 6.30pm.

Super looking forward to this talk from Martin on JavaScript.

Martin heads up manc.js 'A monthly braces embrace in Manchester'.

A great meetup that I'd recommend if you'd like to dive further into JavaScript.

At Manchester FRED, Martin will get hands on with some live coding (brave man!), discuss how to structure your JavaScript, keeping it clean, maintainable and efficient as well as great libraries and ES6 stuff coming in the future.

As usual, please comment :speech_balloon: to sign up for this event so I have an idea of how much pizza / beer to get.
