A Weekend In The Woods

As a web developer spending a lot of time ‘plugged in’ sat in front of a computer, sometimes it’s good to unwind and ‘unplug’.

I recently had a splendid time doing so, my friend Mike http://urbancoppice.co.uk/ was running a course as part of Weekend In The Woods.

Had a great time, staying in a tent in the woods, ate some great food made be Mike’s partner Lynne, including some tasty soup, homemade bread and pizza. Mike is a fantastic teacher and by the end of the weekend everyone had made a cleft gate hurdle.

I found it very refreshing and rewarding to get hands on and practical, would definitely recommend it and will be aiming to go again if it’s on next year.

Thanks to everyone involved for a great weekend in the woods!
