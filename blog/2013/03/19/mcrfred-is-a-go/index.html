McrFRED is a go

Thanks very much to those that have shown interest in the first #McrFRED.

Managed to sell all 30 tickets and there was room for a few more for those of you that also wanted to attend.

Great to see such a quick and positive response! Looking forward to meeting you all.

Thanks again to the wonderful http://manchester.techhub.com for hosting and also providing refreshments.

There will be free WiFi available if you’d like to bring your device of choice.

Hopefully if you are attending you should have received an email with details for the event, if not please let me know and I’ll make sure you get it.

Have also got a #McrFRED email newsletter up and running and you can subscribe to that here:
Signup to the #McrFRED Newsletter

For those of you who can’t make it, if all is well 3/4 Productions will be filming the event and it should be available online.

We’ve also got a new site up at http://fredup.github.com thanks to Brazly for helping out with the logo and site design.

Was also great to get a mention on http://shoptalkshow.com. A great podcast ran by the wonderful Chris Coyier and Dave Rupert.
