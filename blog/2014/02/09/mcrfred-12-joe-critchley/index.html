McrFRED 12 Joe Critchley

McrFRED 12 is now confirmed!

The event will take place on Thursday 27th March, 6.30pm.

At the usual TechHub Manchester.

http://modernizr.com/

Modernizr is a JavaScript library that detects HTML5 and CSS3 features in the user’s browser.

I love Modernizr and it was great last year to have one of the developers Stu Cox at McrFRED.

http://s10wen.com/blog/2013/07/07/mcrfred-04-videos-stu-cox-on-modernizr/

For McrFRED 12 we have @joecritchley.

Joe has been working hard on a new site redesign for Modernizr. I’m really looking forward to seeing what he’s been up to and having a sneak peak of what’s to come.

Joe’s talk will cover how he got started on the project, advancing open-source design, an overview of Modernizr V3, a shift towards content (major UI redesign) and instant user feedback.

Tickets are limited to 40 and will go quick, so please sign up early to avoid disappointment.
