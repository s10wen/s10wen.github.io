McrFRED 50 Matt Claffey

Wowzaz, McrFRED 50! For this one Matt will be talking us through some handy performance wins on a recent project he’s worked on.

Meetup Page

Matt Claffey - @mattclaffey1

Bio

Over the last 6 months I have been focusing mainly around performance for our client and I would like to share some interesting tricks I have learnt along the way. I will be presenting a variety of different topics such as; lazy loading, FOUC vs FOIC, critical-css, resource hints and much, much more!

Thoughout the talk, I will explain what each method is, the best way to implement it and how to measure ‘value’ so you can show your client the positive impact to their website.

Title

Performance: Small tweaks. Big gains
