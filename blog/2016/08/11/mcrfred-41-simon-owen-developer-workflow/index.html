Thanks to everyone that came to the first ‘Together with Google Developers’ and and the positive feedback, it was a lot more work than I originally anticipated so thanks everyone for bearing with me and the positive feedback. Meetup Page

Title

Developer Workflow

Description

Web development is getting increasingly difficult with more devices to test and emerging technologies every day. In this talk I’ll be giving you a sneak peak into my workflow to help aid with the growing complexity of web development, but with a bit of twist. Previous talks I’ve done have been fairly tech heavy, in this talk I’ll also be looking at some of the ideas and non-tech related ideas that I’ve found help me day to day as well.
