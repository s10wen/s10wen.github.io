Naming Convention Ideas for CSS Classes

Naming things is well known for being tricky and causing issues. Here are some ideas to get you going.

There are two hard things in computer science: cache invalidation, naming things, and off-by-one errors.

— Jeff Atwood (@codinghorror) 31 August 2014
  • Countries.
  • Rock Bands.
  • Horror Characters.
  • Spaceship names from SciFi.
  • Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs.
  • Disney Characters.
  • Capitals.
  • Composers.
  • Scientists.
  • Major Winnners from sports.
  • Minions! http://visual.ly/whos-who-minions
  • Star Wars Characters.
  • Star Wars Planets / Systems.
  • Phonetic Alphabet.
  • BBC Gel: http://www.bbc.co.uk/gel/guidelines/typography

Thanks to everyone that contributed on Twitter:

Looking for naming convention ideas. e.g. Canon, Trafalgar. Large, Medium, Small. Giga Mega Alpha. Star Wars characters? Any others you use?
