UpFrontMini 2015

Following the success of @upfrontconf earlier this year and having so many great call for speaker replies we decided to run a smaller event called #upfrontmini.

UpFrontConf

On the 19th May 2015 @upfrontconf was born. With myself and help from Dan, Jack, Rachel and Katie we created a conference that sold out, received much praise and we ended up trending on Twitter in the UK. It was hard work, but with so much positive feedback we were able to create a side event.

UpFrontMini

Thanks again to everyone who came and supported #upfrontmini.

We believed in creating something more accessible for the community from both a speaker and attendee stand point. Perhaps not everyone attending had heard of all the speakers.
