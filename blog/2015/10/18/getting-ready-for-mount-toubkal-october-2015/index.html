Getting Ready For Mount Toubkal October 2015

This week I’m off to Mount Toubkal, here’s a list of things I’m taking and some other useful information.

Thanks to @triblondon for sorting it out and most of the information below.

Trek Information

This trek will take you on a tour of Berber villages before you ascend to the peak of Mount Toubkal (4167m). Over the 4 days, you will have chance to acclimatize to the altitude while spending time in the company of native Berbers and experiencing their culture.

24-27 October. Includes all food, accommodation and mountain guide
http://www.trekkinginmorocco.com/en/treks/walking-treks/berber-villages-and-mt-toubkal-ascent/

Things To Be Aware Of

Vaccinations:
http://www.fitfortravel.scot.nhs.uk/destinations/africa/morocco.aspx#immunisations

Get Passport stamped:
When entering the country, make sure your passport is stamped. Some tourists have experienced difficulties leaving the country because their passport bears no entry stamp.
https://www.gov.uk/foreign-travel-advice/morocco

Make sure travel insurance:

  • Includes coverage for medical evacuation
  • Mountain rescue and coverage for mountaineering up to at least 4,300m.

Weather:
Marrakech ~30C
Toubkal summit will be around -1 to -4
